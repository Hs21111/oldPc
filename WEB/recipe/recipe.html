How to Make the BEST Garlic Herb Pasta 

            Servings: 2         Prep time: 10mins           Cook time: 15-20mins


Ingredients:

  1. Spaghetti - 250 grams
  2. Minced Clove Garlic - 4
  3. Olive Oil - 1/4 cup
  4. Chopped Parsley - 1/4 cup
  5. Chopped Basil - 2 tablespoons
  6. Red Pepper Flakes - 1/4 teaspoon (OPTIONAL)
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Grated Parmesan Cheese - for serving (OPTIONAL)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions until its firm to bite. Reserve about 120 ml of the pasta cooking water before draining.

  2. Saut√© the garlic: While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1-2 minutes, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add herbs and pepper flakes (optional): Stir in the chopped parsley, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using) into the skillet with the garlic. Cook for another 30 seconds until the herbs are fragrant.

  4. Combine pasta and sauce: Drain the cooked pasta and add it directly to the skillet with the garlic and herb mixture. Toss well to coat.

  5. Add pasta water: If the sauce seems too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta cooking water, 1 tablespoon at a time, until it reaches your desired consistency.

  6. Season: Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

  7. Serve: Divide the pasta between two bowls. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired, and serve immediately.
